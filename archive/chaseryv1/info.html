About Me

I am a multifaceted designer and developer. I carry both skills in Graphic Design and Web Development with knowledge spanning from Photoshop to XHTML. Over the last five or so years, I have self taught myself these applications and languages. I excel in XHTML, CSS, Photoshop, and am now developing skills in digital photography along with Illustrator.

As a Graphic Designer, I have a great deal of experience in logo design and web graphics. Photo manipulation and editing I would say is one of my stronger points, I have had multiple designs printed in magazines. I have dealt with print design and understand the requirements to work in that field.

I am gaining a better grasp of the standards within the Web Development field, I am constantly learning and developing new skills in this area. Ultimately I plan on learning at least one of the backend languages, most likely it will be PHP. I feel that it is important to build knowledge and at the same time, this skill will make me even more of a well rounded developer.
