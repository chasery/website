About Ra Optics Snowboard Goggles

Ra Optics was founded by athletes tired of the same products released every year by the typical brands. Our staff was fed up with other brands giving up on providing the innovative gear athletes need to push the threshold of a sport, We believe a new color scheme and graphics does not make the same old products new again. Every one of Ra Optics' products incorporates market-leading innovation. Every one of Ra Optics' products have a purpose. We are proud to offer fellow athletes technology that meets their needs. We are an innovation company.

Ra Optics has been based in the Pacific Northwest since 2009. Our history may be short, but we intend to leave a legacy in the industry.
