Ra Optics Products Snowboard Goggles

Signature Line Snowboard Goggles

The Ra Optics Signature Line. Crafted for superior performance and durability. Inspired by athletes with purpose. Ra Optics engineers form with functionality and technology in every one of its Signature Line Goggles.

Model: 2011

The 2011 Signature Line has a number of innovations over standard goggles. A mirrored photochomic, anti-fog, anti-scratch, ballistic-grade NXT lens technology improves the visual experience of the ride. The silicon lining across the head strap means the goggles will be easily removable even with gloves on. The goggles' spherical shape opens up line of sight in every direction. The straps will be completely modular, allowing for the user to only have to change a portion of the goggles, rather than the entire goggles itself to stay ahead of the latest fashion. Goggles available in black frames, and both gold and silver mirrored lenses. Helmet compatible. Accessory straps coming soon.

Performance Line Snowboard Goggles

Ra Optics' Performance Goggles utilize the latest in lens technology, offering a wide range of benefits to an athlete. Performance Goggles have anti-tunnel vision technology, are embedded with anti-fog protection, and come with an optional transitional lens to handle any lighting conditions.

Model: 2010

The 2010 Performance Line is a tank of a goggles line. The Performance Line's plastic injected frame and mirrored photochromic, anti-fog, anti-scratch, NXT lens means the wearer has the toughest, safest product on the mountain. Helmet compatible. Available in black and white framing, and gold and silver mirroring.

Headwear Snowboard Clothes

Ra Gear line beanies and hats with Smart Polymer Technology. Our beanies and hats respond to an athlete's intensity by absorbing 20 joules of the energy during impact. They does not replace a certified safety product, which typically absorbs 100 joules of energy, but our beanies and hats are better than nothing.

Model: 2010

The 2010 line of beanies and hats integrate our Smart Polymer technology. Ra Optics beanies and hats are washable, breathable and come in a number of fashions and sizes for men, women, and children.
